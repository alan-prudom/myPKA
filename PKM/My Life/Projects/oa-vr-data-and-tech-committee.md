# OA VR Data and Tech Committee

## Why this matters

To provide effective leadership and technical orchestration for the OA Virtual Region as Data and Tech Committee Chair. This project tracks the review of past minutes, coordinates deliverables from working groups and sub-committees, and maintains the weekly Tuesday meeting cycle.

## Meeting Cycle & Agenda

The meetings run weekly on Tuesdays and alternate in a 4-week cycle:

| Week | Meeting Type | Typical Agenda Structure |
|---|---|---|
| **Week 1** | **Data Committee** | 1. Status of regional databases and registration sync<br>2. Data integrity, schema compliance, and privacy audits<br>3. Open floor & new proposals |
| **Week 2** | **Working Group (WG)** | 1. Direct hands-on collaboration on active sprints<br>2. Blockers triage and live debugging<br>3. Assigning sub-tasks |
| **Week 3** | **Tech Sub-committee** | 1. Infrastructure, API performance, and web app hosting updates<br>2. Codebase reviews (e.g., registration toolkit)<br>3. Security reviews and developer environment status |
| **Week 4** | **Working Group (WG)** | 1. Direct hands-on collaboration on active sprints<br>2. Blockers triage and live debugging<br>3. Assigning sub-tasks |
